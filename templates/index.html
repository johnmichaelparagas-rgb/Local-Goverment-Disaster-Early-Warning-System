Capabilities

One portal for the full warning chain

From sensor reading to public broadcast — every step of disaster response in a single, role-secured system.

📡

Real-time hazard monitoring

Track flood, landslide, and storm-surge sensors across municipalities with live readings and per-area status roll-ups.

📝

Field incident reporting

Log localized incidents with multiple hazard photos in a single submit, complete with severity and location detail.

📣

Early-warning broadcasts

Issue colour-coded alerts (Information → Yellow → Orange → Red) targeted to the exact affected municipalities.

⚡

Bulk status operations

Raise or lower hazard status across many sensors at once — by selection, municipality, or hazard type.

🔒

Role-based access control

LGU Admin, Dispatcher, and Public Viewer roles, with brute-force protection and audited actions.

🛡️

Privacy-safe public feed

Dispatcher contacts and reporter details are automatically redacted from public APIs and citizen views.

How it works

Sense. Decide. Warn.

1

Sense

Field sensors and responders feed live hazard readings and incident reports into the portal.

→
2

Decide

Dispatchers and LGU admins assess the situation on a filterable, real-time operations dashboard.

→
3

Warn

Official early warnings are broadcast to affected municipalities and the public situation feed.

Access & roles

The right access for every responder

LGU Admin

Full operational control

  • Create & manage incidents and images
  • Broadcast and cancel warnings
  • Manage users & sensors

Dispatcher

Day-to-day response

  • Update incident status
  • Monitor sensors & warnings
  • Bulk hazard-status updates

Public Viewer

Read-only, privacy-safe

  • View public situation feed
  • See active warnings
  • No access to private contacts
